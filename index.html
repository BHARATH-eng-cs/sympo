// About the Symposium

WHAT IS ZENTHORIX?

ZENTHORIX 2K'26 is the annual national-level technical symposium organized by the Department of Computer Science & Engineering. It is a platform that brings together the sharpest technical minds under one roof.

"Where innovation meets electricity — an experience that charges your intellect and ignites your passion for technology."

From competitive programming to robotics, from paper presentations to design challenges — ZENTHORIX 2K'26 has something electrifying for every tech enthusiast. Join us for a day of knowledge, competition, and networking.
